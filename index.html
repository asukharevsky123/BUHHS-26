About

Built on real Boston data.

ParkBoston pulls together garage rates, public lots, and 6,955 active street meters from City of Boston open data. The street meter map is aggregated into ~600 segments by block, so you can quickly spot where the meters are and how much they cost.

No more circling. No more surprises. Just the spots, the rates, and how to get there.
